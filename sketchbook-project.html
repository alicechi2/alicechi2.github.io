The Sketchbook Project:

To Those Whom it May Concern

The Sketchbook Project is the largest collection of artist sketchbooks collected and distributed by the Brooklyn Art Library. To Those Whom it May Concern is my contribution to this collection.

To Those Whom it May Concern

Project

Created at the peak of the pandemic in 2021, To Those Whom it May Concern is a collection of letters from me to no one in particular and to anyone at all. This sketchbook features unhinged commentary, general life advice, personal anecdotes that probably no one really cares about, and a couple sketches.

To Those Whom it May Concern is currently in possesion of the Brooklyn Art Library. If you happen to be nearby, you can see it in person using the following information:

Title: "To Those Whom it May Concern" - #S8486198

Project: The Sketchbook Project Vol 18
