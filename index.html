About Me

Hi, I'm Alice! I graduated from Harvey Mudd College with a double major in computer science and visual art with some experience in electrical engineering. I currently work as a software engineer at Google and I am interested in working in the intersection of tech and art, particularly in the context of interactive experiences and gaming. I love to create impactful work, anything that connects with and brings joy to others.


I grew up in SoCal and in my spare time I love painting, playing video games, and binge watching Youtube videos. If you want to learn more about me, check out any of my social media platforms or resume linked below and feel free to contact me! :)
