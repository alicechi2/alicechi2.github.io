Flavor Town

Game Development

Flavor Town is a rougelike management game where the player must traverse through procedurally generated maps to fight different ingredients in order to maintain their restaurant and to gain fame and reknown as a chef. Flavor Town is a project worked on by a team of 6 people. For this project I work as the Project Manager, Art Director, and Software Engineer.
